About The Artist

Sofia Barros-Casanova was born and raised in the south of Mexico City and has been living in New Orleans since 2018. She's currently a junior Music Industry student in Loyola University of New Orleans.

Resume

  • Sound Engineering for Nola Sound (2022-present)
  • Session mixing for various student artists
  • Live performances at Berklee College of Music (2019), DIM (2016), etc.

Bands

  • Dopamine (2011-2013)
  • Caligula (2020-2021)
  • Doghaus (2022-present)
